​X Battle: Ek Naya Shooting Game

​X Battle ek naya shooting game hai jo Unity Hub par bana hai. Is game ko khas taur par Indian players ke liye banaya gaya hai. Iska map New Delhi aur India ke dusre hisson ko dikhata hai. Is game ke graphics bilkul realistic hain, jisse aapko sachmuch mein Indian cities mein hone ka experience milega.

​Tournaments aur Gameplay

​X Battle mein all-city tournaments honge. Har team mein 4 players aur 1 extra player honge. Is tournament mein sirf Indian citizens hi bhaag le sakte hain. Is game mein aapka rank "Diamond 2" ya usse upar hona chahiye, aur top-up level 3 ya usse upar. Is game ka HUD (Heads-Up Display) bilkul Free Fire jaisa hai, jisse un players ko bhi aaram se khelne mein madad milegi. Har 6 mahine mein ek bada update aayega.

​Game Launch aur Updates

​Game launch ke baad, pehle 6 mahine mein ye features aayenge:

  • ​AI (Artificial Intelligence): Game mein AI players ko add kiya jayega.
  • ​Discord Coupon: Aapko 5% ka discount coupon milega.
  • ​BGMI Gun Skin: BGMI ki gun skin aapke game mein use ki ja sakti hai.
  • ​BGMI Collab: Game mein BGMI ke sath collaboration hoga.

​Launch ke baad, updates ke liye aap unke Facebook aur YouTube channels ko follow kar sakte hain.

​Nateeja
